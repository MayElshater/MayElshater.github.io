What is Garlic?

Garlic empowers LLM applications to seamlessly integrate contextual advertisements, enabling startups to monetize their AI-driven products effectively. We combine an easy-to-use SDK, powerful ad engine, and real-time analytics to transform conversations into valuable opportunities.

For Startups

Monetize LLM-based platforms with highly relevant, non-intrusive ads directly in AI responses

For Advertisers

Reach ideal audiences with contextually relevant ads within AI-driven conversations

For Users

Experience helpful, relevant ads that enhance rather than interrupt conversations
